Hospitality at UDGAM

We provide FREE accommodation for all participants at SRM University AP Campus. Our goal is to ensure you have a comfortable and hassle-free stay during UDGAM 2025.

All participants will be provided with comfortable accommodation within the SRM AP campus premises at no cost.

Comfortable accommodation in our modern university hostels with all essential amenities provided free of charge.

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Free mess facility

  • Cafeteria
