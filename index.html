Welcome to UDGAM 2025

Where Gaming Dreams Come Alive!

Join us for an epic 4-day celebration of gaming culture, featuring intense competitions, immersive experiences, and unforgettable moments. From esports tournaments to casual gaming, we've got something for every gamer!

5000+ Participants
35+ Universities
40+ Events

  • "Among Us"

    Among Us is a multiplayer game where players work together to complete tasks—unless one of them is secretly an impostor sabotaging the crew. Trust no one and stay vigilant!

  • "Valorant"

    Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter where teams of agents battle to plant or defuse bombs, using unique abilities and precise gunplay. Strategy, teamwork, and sharp aim are key to victory!

  • "BGMI"

    BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) is a battle royale game where players fight to be the last one standing, scavenging for weapons and resources in a vast open world. Survival, strategy, and quick reflexes determine the winner!

  • "MARVEL RIVALS"

    Marvel Rivals is a fast-paced, action-packed game where iconic superheroes and villains clash in epic battles. Collect your favorite characters, unleash powerful abilities, and become the ultimate champion!

  • "FreeFire"

    Free Fire is a battle royale game where 50 players fight to be the last one standing on a shrinking island. Drop in, gear up, and use strategy and skill to outlast your rivals!

  • "Stumble Guys"

    Stumble Guys is a chaotic, multiplayer party game where players race through wacky obstacle courses. Dodge, tumble, and outlast your competitors to be the last one standing!
